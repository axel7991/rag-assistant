FAQ

Common questions

What types of documents can I upload?
Any PDF — contracts, financial reports, research papers, technical manuals, textbooks, and more. If it's a PDF, DocQuery can read it.
How accurate are the answers?
DocQuery only answers from what's actually in your document — it never invents information. Every answer includes source citations so you can verify the exact passage it came from.
Is my data private and secure?
Your documents are stored securely and never used to train AI models. Only you have access to your uploaded files.
How is this different from Ctrl+F?
Ctrl+F finds exact words. DocQuery understands meaning — so asking "what are the payment terms?" will find the relevant clause even if those exact words don't appear together in the document.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — no contracts, no commitments. Cancel anytime and you won't be charged again.
